Pakhtunkhwa Valleys

Swat Valley

Mini Switzerland of Pakistan, filled with lush green landscapes, rivers, and snowy peaks.

Naran Kaghan

Famous for beautiful lakes like Saif-ul-Malook and the breathtaking scenery of the valley.

Chitral Valley

Home to the Kalash Valley, historical sites, and stunning mountains.

Dir Valley

Surrounded by high mountains, beautiful rivers, and cultural heritage.

Shangla Valley

A peaceful valley with mesmerizing views, forests, and cool weather.

Allai Valley

A hidden gem with lush green landscapes, waterfalls, and a peaceful environment.

Torghar Valley

A remote valley with untouched natural beauty and diverse wildlife.

Galiyat Valley

A tourist-favorite region with scenic hill stations like Nathia Gali, Donga Gali, and Ayubia.
